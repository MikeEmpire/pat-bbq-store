## 🎬 Intro Animation (Locked Concept - Train Entry Sequence)

We are replacing generic smoke reveal with:

### 🚂 "Train Arrival" Cinematic Intro

#### Narrative

The train enters the screen like it's pulling into a station.

It establishes:

- Brand presence
- Movement
- Craft
- Personality

It must feel:

- Intentional
- Confident
- Not cartoonish
- Not cheesy
- Premium

### 🎞 Animation Timeline (3.0 seconds max)

#### 0.0 - 0.3s

- Screen is black.
- Very subtle ambient grill sound (optional).
- No UI visible.

#### 0.3 - 1.3s

- The BBQ train slides in from the left.
- Ease-out cubic motion.
- Wheels rotate while moving.
- Slight momentum physics.
- Subtle smoke puffs animate upward.
- Headlight softly glows.
- No background clutter.
- Just the train.

#### 1.3 - 1.8s

- Train settles.
- Slight bounce recoil (5px max).
- Steam dissipates.
- Headlight glow softens.
- Tagline fades in above or below: "Slow Smoked. Perfected."

#### 1.8 - 2.5s

- Logo text fades in: `P-Train's California BBQ`.
- Background transitions from black to hero background.

#### 2.5 - 3.0s

- Train fades down in opacity.
- Homepage hero content fades in.
- Intro complete.

### Important Rules

- Must be skippable.
- Must not block performance.
- Must not delay LCP.
- Must be disabled after first visit (use `localStorage`).
