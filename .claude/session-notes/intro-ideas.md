🚂 Final Intro Animation Concept
“The P Train Arrival”

Tone:
Playful + confident + brand-forward
Not childish. Not over-the-top.

Duration:
2.5–3 seconds max.

🎬 Full Animation Breakdown
0. Black screen (0–400ms)

Subtle train chug sound begins

Very light rumble bass

No music. Just atmosphere.

1. Headlight Glow (400ms–800ms)

Left side of screen:

Circular warm amber light appears

Slight flicker

Growing intensity

The train isn’t visible yet — just anticipation.

2. Train Rolls In (800ms–1600ms)

Cartoon-styled train — but clean vector style.
Not exaggerated proportions.

Animation:

Slides in from left

Slight bounce when it stops (subtle)

Wheels rotate during movement

Light steam puff when it brakes

This is the fun personality moment.

3. Logo Reveal (1600ms–2200ms)

Instead of text coming out literally:

Option A (Best):
The train whistle blows.
Steam puff expands upward.
Inside the steam:
“P Train’s California BBQ” fades in.

Option B:
Train windows glow amber.
Logo fades in above the train.
Soft glow effect.

I recommend Option A.
Steam reveal feels on-brand and fun.

4. Tagline Reveal (2200ms–2500ms)

Under logo:

“Slow Smoked. Family Crafted.”

Subtle fade up.

5. Transition to Homepage (2500ms–3000ms)

Steam expands and becomes:

Background smoke

Then fades into hero section

Smooth transition.
Not hard cut.

🎨 Visual Style Guide

Train Design:

Clean vector SVG

Deep charcoal body

Red wheels

Amber headlight

Subtle shadow

No gradients that feel cheap.
Flat but premium illustration.

Steam:
Soft opacity.
Low density.
Not cartoon cloud puffs.

Sound:
Low volume.
Quick.
Optional mute toggle.

🧠 UX Rules (Important)

✔ Plays only on first visit
✔ Skip button top right after 1 second
✔ Disable on mobile if performance drops
✔ Total under 3 seconds

Premium brands don’t waste time.
